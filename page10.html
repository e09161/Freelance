✨ Exam‑Day Gold Advice

⏱️ Time management

Use the Gold practice tests to get used to timing. For Reading & Use of English, spend 15 minutes on Parts 1‑4 and 45 minutes on the long texts. Leave 5 minutes to transfer answers.

📝 Answer sheet rules

Write in CAPITAL LETTERS for open‑cloze answers. For Listening Part 2, check that your word/phrase fits grammatically. In Writing, stick to the word limit (220‑260 words).

⚠️ Avoid these common mistakes: Ignoring the key word in transformations (Part 4), not reading the two essay bullet points carefully, using informal language in proposals/reports, and spelling errors in Listening Part 2. Practise with the Gold Exam Maximiser to iron these out.
