Charlotte's Web
Kenneth & Alex – Grade 5 “D”

1. Theme – Definition

COMPASSION JUSTICE

Compassion: deep care and the will to help, even when it costs you.
Justice: fairness, standing up for what is right, protecting the vulnerable.

Together they drive the whole story – Fern’s bold stand and Charlotte’s selfless friendship.

2. Theme – Quotes

"The pig couldn't help being born small, could it? If I had been very small at birth, would you have killed me?"
– Fern (Justice)
"You have been my friend. That in itself is a tremendous thing."
– Charlotte (Compassion)

Two voices, one message: every life has value.

3. Theme – Textual Evidence

⚖️

Fern’s protest

She confronts her father and saves Wilbur’s life (Chapter 1).

🕸️

Charlotte’s web

She writes “SOME PIG” to change how others see him (Chapter 11).

🥚

Wilbur’s turn

He protects Charlotte’s egg sac – paying compassion forward (Chapter 21).

Every step proves the theme: justice saves Wilbur, compassion keeps him alive, and gratitude passes it on.

4. Global Issue – Definition & Impact

BULLYING

Bullying is repeated, unfair treatment of someone seen as weaker or different.
It marginalises children – just like Wilbur before Fern and Charlotte stood up for him.

Impact: lower self‑esteem, fear, loneliness. But upstanders can change everything.
